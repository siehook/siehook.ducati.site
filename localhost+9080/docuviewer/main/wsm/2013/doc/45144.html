Removal of the primary drive gear
Drain the engine oil (Changing engine oil and filter cartridge).
Remove the oil sump guard (Removing the oil sump guard).
Remove the clutch cover (Removing the clutch cover).
Remove the clutch unit (Removing the clutch).
Withdraw the clutch housing (1) complete with driven gear of the primary pair (A).
Remove the inner spacer (2).
Remove the oil pump (C) (Removing the oil pump).
Lock the primary pinion (B) with the holding tool (E) no. 88713.3417 and unscrew the threaded ring nut (3) using the bushing (F) no. 88713.3406.
Remove the nut (3) and lockwasher (4).
Remove the complete primary drive gear (B) using a commercial puller and placing a brass or aluminium pad between the end of the crankshaft and the puller screw.
Remove the spacer (D).
