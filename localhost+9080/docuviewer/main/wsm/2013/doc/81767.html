Refitting the number plate holder
Check the presence of clips (9) on the number plate holder (5).
Refit tail light (10) on number plate holder (5) and tighten the screws (8) to a torque of 3 Nm ±10%.
Connect the rear turn indicator connectors (C) to the main wiring connectors (D).
When connecting connectors (C) and (D) pay utmost attention to comply with the ID tags on cables: RH turn indicator wiring section (F) and LH turn indicator wiring section (G) can be identified by their tags, "DX" and "SX" respectively.
Connect the wiring fast-on connectors (E) to the number plate light reeds on the splashguard (6).
Lay wiring between pins on number plate holder.
Fit the splashguard (6) on the number plate holder (5), paying attention not to press the wires underneath.
Tighten the screws (7) to a torque of 3 Nm ±10%.
Connect rear turn indicator wiring section (H) and tail light wiring section (L), respectively to rear turn indicator wiring (M) and tail light wiring (N) coming from number plate holder assembly (5).
Fasten wirings together at the connectors, as shown.
Set number plate holder assembly (5) in place, placing previously tied connectors between LH side body panel (P) and rear grab rail (Q).
Pay attention in the process and avoid rubbing connector cables against LH subframe (R) since they could get damaged.
Make sure that the vibration dampers (2) are present.
Fix the number plate holder (5) by starting the screws (11) and (1).
Tighten the screws (11) to a torque of 6 Nm ±10% on screws (1).
Fit the plate (12) on the number plate holder (5) and tighten the screws (3) to a torque of 6 Nm ±10%.
Connect rear wiring (B) to the wiring and position clamps (A).
Refit side panniers, if any, and top case, if any, to the corresponding mounts.
Refit the rear left side body panel (Refitting the side body panels and tail guard).
Refit the seat (Refitting the seat).
