Refitting the cooling system hoses and unions
Install an O-ring (12) in the seat of the horizontal cylinder head union (11) and one in the vertical cylinder head union seat (10).
Fix union (11) on the horizontal cylinder head with screws (9) smeared with specified threadlocker, and tighten them to 6 Nm (Min. 5 Nm - Max. 7 Nm).
Fix union (10) on the vertical cylinder head with screws (9) smeared with specified threadlocker, and tighten them to 6 Nm (Min. 5 Nm - Max. 7 Nm).
