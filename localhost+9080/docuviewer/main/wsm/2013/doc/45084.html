Refitting the intake manifold and coolant union
Apply specified threadlocker on the union (3), insert it in place with its sealing washer (4) and tighten the union to 2.5 Nm (Min. 2 Nm - Max. 3 Nm).
Install the pipe (B) and tighten the clamp (A) to 1 Nm ±10%.
Check that the mating surfaces of the cylinder head and manifold are perfectly flat and clean and install the intake manifold (1) on the cylinder head.
Tighten the retaining screws (2) to a torque of 10 Nm (Min. 9 Nm - Max. 11 Nm), in a crossed pattern (1‑2‑3‑4).
Refit the cylinder head cover (Refitting the camshafts).
Connect the coils.

Fix the timing belt covers (Refitting the timing outer covers).
