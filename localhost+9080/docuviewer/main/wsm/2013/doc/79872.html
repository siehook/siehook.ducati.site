Refitting airbox and throttle body
Reassembling airbox – throttle body
If removed, fit the sealing washers (10) in the seat (M) of the airbox (4).
If removed, insert the sealing washers (11) in the seats (K) of the intake funnels (9).
If removed, refit intake funnels (9) on throttle body (8), apply recommended threadlocker to the screws (12) and tighten to 2.5 Nm ±10% following the indicated sequence.
After tightening, visually check that seals (11), previously fitted on the intake funnels, are still inside their seats and that the intake funnels are perfectly against the throttle body.
Fix throttle body (8) to airbox (4).
Apply the recommended threadlocker to the screws (7).
Start and position retaining screws (7) to 5 Nm ±10%, following the sequence shown in the figure.
If removed, position the horizontal map sensor (7) on airbox (1), by matching the indicated profiles.
Insert the spacer with collar (13) in the hole of the horizontal map sensor as shown in the figure.
Fix the horizontal map sensor by starting screw (2) and tightening it to 5 Nm ±10%.
If the drain hose (15) has been previously removed, place clamp (14) on it.
Fit the drain hose (15) by inserting it fully home on union (I) on airbox (4).
Position clamp (14) as shown.
Secure drain hose (15) in place with clamp (14).
Apply specified grease to the O-ring (16).
Insert the O-ring fully in the relevant seat (L) of airbox (4).
If the secondary air system pipe has been removed from airbox (1), position spring clamp (17) on the secondary air system pipe (18).
Fit the secondary air system pipe (18) on airbox (1), so that curve (N), highlighted in yellow, is near recess (P), as shown.
Block the secondary air system pipe (18) using spring clamp (17).
Fit another spring clamp (17) in the "free" end of the secondary air system pipe (18), indicated by the arrow.
If removed, refit the throttle cables as follows: install the opening (F) and closing (E) cables inside their seats on the throttle plate body (8) plate (L) as shown in the figure.
Note
The throttle cables can be distinguished thanks to the writing of different colour on them:
- the opening throttle cable features the white writing;
- the closing throttle cable features the yellow writing.
Insert the bolts (Q) in the corresponding seats of the pulley. Keep oriented as shown in figure, fasten the cables (F) and (E) by inserting the nuts (D).
Tighten nuts (D) to a torque of 2.5 Nm ±10%.
Position dust seals (S) on throttle cable sheath ends.
Insert the throttle closing (E) and opening (F) cable inside the cable ring (M) on the airbox (4).
