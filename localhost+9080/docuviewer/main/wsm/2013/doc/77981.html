Installation of the headlight fairing
Reassembling the headlight fairing
If previously disassembled, reassemble the headlight fairing.
Fasten windscreen rail (21) to its fixed mount (22) using springs (20).
Warning
Springs must be installed so that the ends are positioned as shown.
Position the windscreen rail (21) on its fixed mount (22), making sure that tabs (A) on bottom end of rail (21), engage under tabs (B) on mount (22), as indicated by the arrows.
Complete the operation by inserting rail (21) slots on threaded pins (C) of mount (22).
Install washers (19) on threaded pins (C) of mount (22).
Fit slider (15) fully home against mount (22), oriented as indicated, making sure that slots on sliding shoe (15) match with the references (E) on mount (22).
Assemble support unit (18) to headlight fairing (4), ensuring rail (15) and threaded pins (C) of windscreen assembly match the slots on headlight fairing, as shown.
Insert rail (15) in slot (F) on headlight fairing (4), at the previously installed rail, oriented as shown.
Fit the rubber block (16) fully home on screw (14).
Start screw (14) on support assembly (18), in its thread on rail (15) (do not tighten).
Slide the support assembly (18) fully home, in the direction indicated by the arrow.
Move support lock (21) in the direction indicated by the black arrow.
Apply specified threadlocker on the pin thread (C).
Start the special screws (17) on threaded pins (C), centring their collar into previously-installed washers (19).
Tighten the screw (14) and screws (17) to a torque of 3 Nm ± 10%.
Install immobilizer antenna (13) on headlight fairing (4), and tighten the self-tapping screws (12) to 0.7 Nm ± 10%.
Position the immobilizer antenna cable (G) as indicated, sliding it inside opening (H) on headlight fairing.
Manually start the silent-bocks (23) on instrument panel (11).
Install safety washers (24) onto silent-blocks (23).
Install instrument panel, position it as shown, on headlight fairing (4) and start nuts (10).
Tighten nuts (10) to a torque of 3 Nm ± 10%.
Position the windscreen (7) with rubber blocks (10) on headlight fairing (4) and start screws (8).
Tighten the screws (8) to a torque of 3 Nm ± 10%.
Installation of the headlight fairing
Connect instrument panel connector (6) and refit headlight fairing (4) to the vehicle by engaging slot (A) in rubber block (5) on headlight.
Start and tighten the screws (3) to a torque of 10 Nm ± 10%, on either side of the headlight subframe.
Start the tank fairing screws (2) and tighten them to a torque of 2 Nm ± 10%.
Connect the Immobilizer antenna connector (1).
Refit the front half-fairing (Refitting the front half-fairing) and the left and right-hand side panel covers (Refitting the panel).
