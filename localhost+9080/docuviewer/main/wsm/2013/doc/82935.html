Solenoid starter
Remove the rider seat (Removing the seat).
The solenoid starter (1) is elastically mounted to the battery mount.
Disconnect the battery (Battery).
Remove the protection cover (A).
Undo screws (2), taking care to collect the spring washers (3).
Remove the starter motor-solenoid starter cable (4) and the solenoid starter-battery cable (5).
Disconnect the wiring connector (6) of the solenoid starter from the wiring.
Remove solenoid starter (1) by sliding it upwards.
Checking operation of the solenoid starter
To check the solenoid starter follow the instructions under Section "Starter motor".
Ensure that the terminals are not oxidised and apply water repellent spray.
Position the solenoid starter-starter motor cable (4) and the solenoid starter-battery cable (5) on the solenoid starter terminals.
Start screws (2) fitting the spring washers (3).
Tighten screws (2) to a torque of 4 Nm Â±10%.
Refit the protection cap (A).
Connect connector (6) between the solenoid starter and the battery wiring harness (Battery).
Engage solenoid starter (1) in tabs (B) to the battery mount.
Refit the rider seat (Refitting the seat).
