Important
Do not use any additives in fuel or lubricants. Using them could result in severe damage to the engine and motorcycle components.
Warning
Vehicle is compatible only with fuels with a max. ethanol content of 10% (E10). Using fuel with ethanol content over 10% is forbidden. Using it could result in severe damage to the engine and motorcycle components. Using fuel with ethanol content over 10% will make the warranty null and void.
Engine oil
A good quality engine oil has special properties. Use only a highly detergent engine oil with certified SE, SF or SG or higher service ratings as marked on the container.
