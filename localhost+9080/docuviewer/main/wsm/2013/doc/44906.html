Removing the lubrication system
Remove the oil sump guard (Removing the oil sump guard).
Remove the front half-fairings (Removing the front half-fairing).
Drain the system (Changing engine oil and filter cartridge).
Disconnect the oil pressure switch (1) from the main wiring and recover washer (2).
Open the hose clip (3).
Undo screw (4) of plate (5) and remove the latter.
Slide off crankcase half pipes (6) paying attention to their O-Rings.
Upon disassembly, make sure not to lose the O-rings (A) that guarantee the coupling sealing.
Undo and remove screw (7) with spacer (8) that secures the cooler to the headlight support.
Slide the cooler out of headlight support pin (B).
Loosen nuts (D) of pipes (6) from nipples (9), and disconnect them from the radiator.
Loosen nipples (9) on the radiator, and collect gaskets (10).
