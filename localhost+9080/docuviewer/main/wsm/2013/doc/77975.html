Removal of the panel
Removal of the LH panel
Remove fusebox cover (1) by removing the tabs (A) from the slits on LH panel cover.
Loosen screws (2) and (3) and remove LH panel cover (4), by lifting the rear part to unhook the tabs (B) from the slots (C) in the headlight fairing.
Removal of the RH panel
Open the glove compartment cover (5).
Loosen screws (2) and (3) and remove RH panel cover (6), by lifting the rear part to unhook the tabs (D) from the slots (E) in the headlight fairing.
Lift cover (5) to reach screw (3).
Remove the pin (8) thereby releasing the curved end between tabs (F).
Remove pin (8) from holes on RH panel cover (6) and glove compartment cover (5), collecting spring (9).
Note
To help pin removal, keep glove compartment cover open.
Remove the glove compartment cover (5).
