Refitting the handlebar
Refitting the handlebar
If the spacers (13) were removed from the steering head, lubricate with silicone spray.
Seat the spacers (13) in the bores in the steering head, aiming them as shown in the figure.
Upon completion of the operation, clean all excess lubricant from the components.
Apply the recommended grease to the underside of the screws (10).
Insert the screws (10) on the stud bolts (11).
Fit the toothed washers (12) on screws (10) protruding end.
Apply the recommended threadlocker on the thread in view of screws (10).
Fit stud bolts (11) starting screws (10).
Tighten the screws (10) to a torque of 45 Nm ± 5%, following a 1-2-1 sequence.
Position handlebar (9) in its seat on the stud bolts (11).
Reference points (A) on handlebar must be at the same distance from stud bolts (11) and flush with the stud bolt surface.
Smear the screws (7) with specified grease.
Refit the U-bolt (8) and insert the screws (7).
Tighten the screws (7) to a torque of 25 Nm ± 5%, following a 1-2-3-4 -3 sequence, as shown.
Refitting the handgrips
Lubricate handlebar (9) on the seat of the removed handgrips, using recommended lubricant and install the LH and RH handgrips (6) and the relevant counterweights (5), tightening them to 2 Nm ± 10%.
On the left handgrip, tighten the two retaining screws (15) to the handlebar.
Connect LH (16) and RH connectors (17) of the heated grips and correctly tie the wiring, as described in "Routing of wiring on frame" for the base version or Section "Routing of wiring on frame" for the Pikes Peak, Granturismo or Touring versions.
Refit the hydraulic unit (Q). Fit the spot light switch (19) on handlebar left-hand side (9), until tab (R) is against clutch master cylinder control.
Start screws (18) to fasten device.
Tighten the screws (18) to a torque of 1.3 Nm ±10%.
Position the left-hand switch (3), connect connector to main wiring and tighten screws (4) to the torque of 1.3 Nm ± 10%.
Refit the right-hand switch (1), connect connector to main wiring and tighten screws (2) to the torque of 1.3 Nm ± 10%.
Refit front brake control, throttle control and clutch hydraulic control.
