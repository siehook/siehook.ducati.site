Closing the crankcase
If removed, apply threadlocker on screw (7), insert it with washer (8) on the crankcase half and tighten it to a torque of 8 Nm (Min. 7 Nm - Max. 9 Nm).
If previously removed, apply specified threadlocker on dowel thread (9), and tighten to a torque of 20 Nm (Min. 18 Nm - Max. 22 Nm).
If previously removed, apply specified threadlocker on plug (10), and tighten to a torque of 24 Nm (Min. 21 Nm - Max. 27 Nm).
Install O-ring (6) in its seat (B) in the crankcase half.
Fit the hose (5) in the crankcase half, by inserting the end (C) fully in the O-ring. Align the hole (A) of the hose with the threaded hole (D) in the crankcase half.
Apply threadlocker to the screw (4) and start it in the threaded hole (M).
Tighten the screw to a torque of 8 Nm (Min. 7 Nm - Max. 9 Nm).
Install the timing layshaft (2) in the needle roller bearing in the clutch-side crankcase half.
To avoid damaging the sealing ring (12) on the timing layshaft, protect the threaded end of the shaft with the special protective cap (X) no. 88713.3520. Dampen sealing ring (12) with alcohol and fit it on the timing layshaft. Push seal until it is against the roller bearing (11).
Fit the snap ring (1) in the groove on the shaft and remove the protective cap.
When refitting used components, before installing the gearbox assembly in the crankcase half, make sure that the inner races (C) of the gearbox shaft bearings are fitted into the correct bearings and have not been left on the shafts.
Match gearbox shafts, fit the shims and fit them to the clutch-side crankcase half.
A table and a list of the gearbox components are indicated in Section "Reassembling the gearbox shafts".
Insert the forks of 1st - 4th and 2nd - 3rd speed (F) in the corresponding sliding slots of the secondary shaft driven gears and insert the pin (G) in the forks: the two forks must be fitted with the number that indicates the gear facing upwards.
Note
The two selector forks are identical.
Insert the fork of 5th - 6th speed (H) in the primary shaft driven gear and the pin (G) in the fork (H): the fork must be fitted with the number indicating the gear facing the crankcase half.
Position pin (G) of forks (F) in the crankcase half.
Holding the gear lever claw, position the gear drum (I) in the crankcase half.
Position the pin (G) of the fork (E) in the crankcase half.
Install the crankcase with calculated shims in the crankcase half bearing: the shims (P) of the shaft must be placed with the chamfering facing the shaft.
Position the connecting rods (Q) matching the corresponding seats of the cylinder.
Important
Make sure that the connecting rods are correctly positioned in the relevant cylinder seats. Incorrect positioning of the connecting rods at this stage will inevitably lead to the need to re-open the crankcase half.
Check that the two centring bushes (13) are correctly fitted.
Grease the O-ring (14) to hold it in position and install it.
Apply a uniform and continuous bead (R) of DUCATI sealing compound to the mating surfaces of the crankcase halves, going around all the holes as shown in the figure.
Bring the crankcase halves (15) and (16) together, tapping with a rubber mallet in the area of the shafts, if necessary.
Apply the recommended grease to the screws and insert them in the crankcase halves (note that the screws feature different lengths). Start with the larger diameter screws (M8), and follow the order indicated.

Before installing the crankshaft grease the sliding tracks with the specified product to avoid seizing the bearing crankshaft.
Tighten all screws to the torque specified below:
-
screws (3) and (17) with a preload of 9 Nm (Min. 17 Nm - Max. 21 Nm); then tighten to 25 Nm (Min. 22 Nm - Max. 28 Nm);
-

Check that the crankshaft can be turned with a certain amount of interference with the main bearings (the crankshaft should have a pre-load of 0.20 to 0.30 mm); check also that all the parts you have fitted are free to rotate or move correctly.
Install the cover to reach the mesh filter (Changing engine oil and filter cartridge).
Install the cartridge filter (Changing engine oil and filter cartridge).
Fit the sealing ring (20) on the gear secondary shaft with side (L) to the engine side.
Install the bush of tool (Y) no. 88713.2060 on the secondary shaft. Lubricate the new ring (20) and seat it.
Using the drift, drive the sealing ring fully against the crankcase half bearing.
