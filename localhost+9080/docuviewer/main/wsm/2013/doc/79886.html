Refitting the exhaust system
Place the vertical exhaust manifold (9) with gasket (8) on the vertical cylinder.
Tighten retaining nuts (6) to a torque of 10 Nm ±10%.
If previously removed, tighten the vertical lambda sensor (5) to 24.5 Nm ±10%.
Place the horizontal exhaust manifold (7) with gasket (8) on the horizontal cylinder.
Tighten retaining nuts (6) to a torque of 10 Nm ±10%.
If previously removed, tighten the horizontal lambda sensor (5) to 24.5 Nm ±10%.
Position the catalytic converter (3) and start screw (4) on the vehicle left side.
Support the catalytic converter and start screw (4) on the vehicle right side.
Fit the two fixing springs (2).
Tighten the two retaining screws (4) to a torque of 10 Nm ±10%.
Lay control cable (1) in plate (B).
Turn pulley (A) of the exhaust valve to facilitate the insertion of the control cable (1).
Tighten the retaining nut (C) to a torque of 2.5 Nm ±10%.
If previously removed, insert plugs (11) in copper washers (10), start the plugs in the vertical (9) and horizontal (7) head exhaust manifolds and tighten them to 25 Nm ±10%.
