Removal of the tank fairings
First remove the left and right-hand side panel covers (Removing the panel) and front half-fairing (Removing the front half-fairing).
Loosen screws (1) retaining headlight fairing to tank fairing.
Loosen screws (2) and collect relevant washers.
Loosen screws (3) and collect relevant nylon washers.
Loosen the screws (4).
Remove the LH side (5) and RH side (6) tank fairing.
Undo the screws (3) and remove central tank fairing (7), collecting the relevant washers.
