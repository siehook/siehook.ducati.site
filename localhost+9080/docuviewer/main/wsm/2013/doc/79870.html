Remove the right air duct (Removing the air filters).
Remove connector (A) from the horizontal head air pressure sensor (1).
Undo and remove screw (2) retaining the horizontal head air pressure sensor (1).
Work on the vehicle right side to remove connector (8) of the APS sensor.
Work on the vehicle left side to remove connector (C) of the TPS/ETV motor.
Loosen clamp (3).
Loosen clamp (3).
Slide off the airbox (4).
Work on both throttle control cables (E) and (F), loosen nuts (D) and rotate pulley (G) of the throttle body to slide off the cables.
Removing the fuel injectors
Work on both intake manifolds and remove connectors (H) of the injector main wiring.
Work on both intake manifolds, then loosen and remove the two screws retaining the fuel pipes (I).
Work on both intake manifolds and remove the two injectors (5).
Refitting the injectors
Work on both intake manifolds and check the presence of O-rings (6) and (P) on injectors (5). Apply specified grease on O-rings (6) and (P) of injectors (5), having care not leave residues on the injector surfaces and using only the quantity of product needed to perform the following assembly operations. Insert injector (5) in union (R) of the fuel piping.
Important
To avoid damaging the injector O-rings, insert the injectors completely inside the unions by keeping them in line with their seat.
Insert injectors (5) in their seat on the intake manifolds so that notches (T) are correctly positioned as shown.
Important
To avoid damaging the O-ring, insert the injectors completely inside the intake manifolds by keeping them in line with their seat.
Keep unions pressed against the injectors, then start and tighten screws (I) fully home.
Tighten retaining screw (I) to 5 Nm ±10% making sure that notches (T) remain in the position indicated before.
Connect connectors (H) of the main wiring to the injectors.
Disassembling airbox – throttle body
