Installation of the water pump
Clean the seat in the cover, any parts you intend to reuse, and the impeller shaft. Lubricate with engine oil and refit as follows.
Using drift (C) no. 88713.0869 fit the new mechanic seal (9) on the impeller shaft.
Working on cover internal side, insert the inner spacer (15) with the sharp edge (D) outwards. Using a suitable drift working on the outer ring, drive bearing (13) - with the shielded side (E) facing the cover - fully home on the spacer (15).
Fit the other bearing (12), driving it fully home on bearing (13).
Apply the recommended threadlocker on the screws (10).
Fit the two screws (10) with washer (11), and tighten them to 10 Nm (Min. 9 Nm - Max. 11 Nm).
Turn the cover upside down and, using drift part (F) no. 88713.0870, drive the counter-washer (14) fully home on the spacer (15).
The white side of counter-washer (14) must be facing upwards.
Fit the impeller shaft (6) with the previously fitted mechanical seal (9).
Insert spacer (8), aimed as shown in the figure, and lock it in place with snap ring (7).
Turn the impeller (6) and make sure that it can rotate freely, without excessive resistance.
Clean the mating surface thoroughly on the pump cover and on the generator cover.
Apply a bead of sealing compound to the water pump cover (3), as shown in the figure.
Fit the centring dowels (5) and position cover (3) on the generator cover.
Insert the screws (1).
Apply specified threadlocker on screw (16) and start it.
Apply specified threadlocker on screw (2) and start it.
Tighten the screws to a torque of 10 Nm (Min. 9 Nm - Max. 11 Nm), following the tightening sequence specified in the figure.
After tightening, remove any excessive threadlocker.
Refit the generator cover (Installing the generator cover).
Refit the pump-cylinder hoses (Refitting the cylinder/piston assembly).
Refit the clutch slave cylinder (Refitting the clutch slave cylinder).
Fill the system with engine oil (Changing engine oil and filter cartridge).
Refit the sprocket cover (Refitting the front sprocket).
Fit the cooling system hoses on the water pump cover (Refitting the cooling system hoses and unions).
Fill the cooling system (Changing the coolant).
Fit the half-fairings (Refitting the tank fairings).
Fit the front half-fairings (Refitting the front half-fairing).
