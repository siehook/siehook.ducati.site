Refitting the side plates
Plate refitting sequence is as follows.
Smear all indicated parts with recommended grease and tighten them as follows:
-
tighten nut (1) to a torque of 50 Nm ± 5% while holding the pin (2);
-
tighten nut (3) to a torque of 50 Nm ± 5% while holding the pin (4);
-
tighten the screws (5) to a torque of 25 Nm ± 5%, in a cross pattern;
-
tighten the special screw (6) to a torque of 50 Nm ± 5%;
-
tighten the special screw (7) to a torque of 50 Nm ± 5%;
-
tighten nut (8) to a torque of 50 Nm ± 5% while holding the pin (9).
Pre-assemble clearance adjuster (10).
Use recommended grease and torque wrench with tool part no. 88713.3526, and tighten ring nut (11) to 100 Nm ± 5%, while holding the clearance adjuster (10).
Applying recommended grease, tighten the special screw (12) to a torque of 50 Nm ± 5%.
Pre-assemble clearance adjuster (13).
Use recommended grease and torque wrench with tool part no. 88713.3526, and tighten ring nut (14) to 100 Nm ± 5%, while holding the clearance adjuster (13).
Applying recommended grease, tighten the special screw (15) to a torque of 50 Nm ± 5%.
Applying recommended threadlocker, tighten the screw (16) to a torque of 42 Nm ± 5%.
Applying recommended threadlocker, tighten the screw (17) to a torque of 42 Nm ± 5%.
Install the rear subframe(Refitting the rear subframe).
Connect wiring connectors on the engine (Routing of wiring on frame) for the base version.
Connect wiring connectors on the engine (Routing of wiring on frame) for the Pikes Peak, Granturismo and Touring versions.
Refit the centre stand, if any, (Refitting the centre stand).
Refit the side stand (Refitting the side stand).
Refit the front sprocket (Refitting the front sprocket).
Refit the clutch slave cylinder (Refitting the clutch slave cylinder).
Refit the gearchange control (Refitting the gearchange control).
Connect the starter motor/solenoid starter cable (Refitting the starter motor).
Refit the lower screw retaining water radiator to engine (Refitting the water radiator).
Refit the cooling system pipes and fittings on engine (Removing the cooling system pipes and unions).
Refill the cooling system (Changing the coolant).
Refit the oil cooler hoses to the engine (Refitting the lubrication system).
Fill the lubrication system (Changing engine oil and filter cartridge).
Refit the exhaust system (Refitting the exhaust system).
Connect the caps to the spark plugs. (Replacing the spark plugs)..
Refit the injectors and fuel system to intake manifolds (Refitting filter box and throttle body).
Refit the secondary air valve and hoses (Refitting the secondary air system).
Refit the oil breather hose, blow-by, throttle body and filter box (Refitting the filter box and throttle body).
Connect the throttle control cable (Refitting the throttle control).
Refit the air filter (Refitting the air filters).
Refit the fuel tank (Refitting the fuel tank).
Refit the instrument panel internal covers (Refitting the panel).
Refit the oil sump guard unit (Refitting the oil sump guard).
Refit the half-fairings (Refitting the tank fairings).
Refit the front half-fairings (Refitting the front half-fairing).
Refit the side body panels (Refitting side body panels and tail guard).
Refit the seats (Refitting the seat).
