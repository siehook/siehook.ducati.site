Refitting the gearchange mechanism
Apply the threadlocker on pivot (1).
Install O-ring (3) onto pivot (1).
Insert the pivot (1) in the gearchange lever (2), and insert the other O-ring (3) and the washer (4).
Fit the gearchange lever (1) on the footpeg plate inserting and tightening the pivot (1) to the specified torque 23±10%.
Fit lever (7) on gear transmission rod assembly (8), starting screw (9).
Tighten screw (9) to a torque of 10 Nm ±10%.
Fit the gearchange control unit inserting the lever (7) on the gear control pivot, apply threadlocker on the screw (6) and insert it on the lever (7).
Tighten screw (6) to a torque of 10 Nm ±10%.
