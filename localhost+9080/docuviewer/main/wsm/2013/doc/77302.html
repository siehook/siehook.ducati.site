Installation of the fuel tank filler cap lock
With cap closed, open flap to reach lock seat.
Using a suitable tool, press on the surface indicated by the arrow and partially lift the central body.
Remove tool and fully open the central body (until it clicks).
Using a suitable tool, push on bolt surface to bring it fully home inside cap.
Hold it like this until lock is in place.
Insert one of the two supplied keys in the lock and use the indicated notch as a reference for correct insertion position.
The indicated notch must be on cap LH side.
Fully insert lock in the cap and ensure the lock is flush with the painted aluminium cap surface.
Release bolt.
Try to open/close cap a few times to ensure it works.
Close cap and remove key.
Close flap.
