Removal of the engine
In order to remove engine you must first remove a series of other components from the motorcycle.
Most of these removal procedures are described in the relative sections of this manual.
Following is the logical sequence in which the parts are to be removed from the motorcycle and gives a reference to the section of the manual in which the removal procedure is described.
This section describes only the operations to be carried out after having removed all the parts listed in the flow chart.
Remove the seats (Removing the seat).
Remove the side body panels (Removing side body panels and tail guard).
Remove the front half-fairings (Removing the front half-fairing).
Remove the half-fairings (Removing the tank fairings).
Remove the oil sump guard (Removing the oil sump guard).
Remove the instrument panel internal covers (Removing the panel).
Remove the fuel tank (Removing the fuel tank).
Remove the air filter (Removing the air filters).
Disconnect the throttle control cable (Removing the throttle control).
Remove the filter box, the throttle body, the blow-by and the oil breather pipe (Removing filter box and throttle body).
Remove the secondary air system valve and pipes (Removing the secondary air system).
Remove supply system and injectors from the intake manifolds (Removing filter box and throttle body).
Disconnect the caps from the spark plugs.
Remove the exhaust system (Removing the exhaust system).
Drain the lubrication system (Changing engine oil and filter cartridge).
Remove the cooler oil hoses from the engine block (Removing the lubrication system).
Drain the cooling system (Changing the coolant).
Remove the cooling system hoses and unions from the engine block (Removing the cooling system hoses and unions).
Remove the water radiator lower fastener from the engine (Removing the water radiator).
Disconnect the solenoid starter/starter motor cable (Refitting the starter motor).
Remove the gearchange control (Removing the gearchange control).
Remove the clutch slave cylinder (Removing the clutch slave cylinder).
Remove the front sprocket cover (Removing the front sprocket).
Remove the side stand (Removing the side stand).
Remove the central stand, if any (Removing the central stand)
Disconnect wiring connectors on the engine block.
Install the engine support no. 88713.3396 under the engine to support it upon its removal from the frame.
Loosen nuts (1) on the left side of the frame, in correspondence to the two engine upper supporting pins (2) and (3).
Loosen the screws (4).
Hold the swinging arm shaft screw (5) on the bike LH side, and loosen at the same time screw (5) on the opposite side.
Use drift no. 88713.1074 to completely slide out the swinging arm shaft (8) and recover washer (6) on the left side.
In this way the swinging arm is not fixed to the engine any more, and can be left on the frame.
Remove upper support screws (2) and (3).
Withdraw the complete engine assembly from the frame by lowering it and pushing it forwards.
If necessary, remove the frame lateral plates following the procedure described in Section "Removing the lateral plates".
