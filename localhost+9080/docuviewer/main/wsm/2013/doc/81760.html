Removing the number plate holder
Remove the seat (Removing the seat).
Remove the rear left side body panel (Removing the side body panels and tail guard)
Remove the side panniers from their mounts (Side pannier kit).
Loosen screws (1) retaining number plate holder and keep the vibration dampers (2).
Remove clamps (A), and disconnect tail light connector (B) from the main wiring.
Undo the screws (3) to remove the plate (4) from the number plate holder (5).
Separate the number plate holder (5) from the splashguard (6), by loosening the three screws (7).
Lift the splashguard (6) from the number plate holder (5), taking care to the inner wiring.
Disconnect the wiring fast-on connectors (E) from the number plate light reeds.
Remove the clips (F) from the rear turn indicator connectors (C), and disconnect connectors (C) from main wiring connectors (D).
Loosen screws (8) and remove the tail light from number plate holder (5).
