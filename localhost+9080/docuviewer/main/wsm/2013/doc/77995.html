Refitting the side body panels and tail guard
Fit tail guard shells (9) and (10) and start top screws (11) and (12), and bottom screws (8) as well.
Tighten the screws (8) to a torque of 10 Nm ± 10% and the screws (11) and (12) to a torque of 5 Nm ± 10%.
Fit the RH side (4) and LH side (5) body panel to the motorcycle.
Fit the RH and LH side body panel and start screws (3) and (2), with washers (6) and (7).
Tighten the screws (2) to a torque of 4 Nm ± 10% and the screws (3) to a torque of 2 Nm ± 10%.
Refit the rider seat (1).
