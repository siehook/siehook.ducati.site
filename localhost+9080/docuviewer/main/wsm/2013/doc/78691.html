Removing the ABS control unit
Remove the seat (Removing the seat).
Drain off the hydraulic fluid from brake system hoses, by disconnecting these from the calliper and master cylinder (Changing the front brake system fluid - Changing the rear brake system fluid).
Remove ABS pipe cover (2) by undoing the retaining screws (1).
Disconnect ABS control unit (4) connector (3), lift unit from the vehicle.
Loosen the four special screws (5) and (6) retaining pipes (7), (8), (9) and (10), on ABS control unit (4), by removing the sealing washers (11).
Warning
Whenever removed, seals must be renewed on reassembly.
Remove the ABS control unit (4).
Important
Do not open the ABS control unit: if faulty, replace it.
Should it be necessary to replace one or several hoses, refer to “Routing wiring harnesses / hoses” for the base version or to “Routing wiring harnesses / hoses” for the Pikes Peak, Granturismo or Touring versions.
