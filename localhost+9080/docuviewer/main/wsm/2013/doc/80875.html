Refitting the rear subframe
Install RH (2) and LH (3) subframe halves on the plates.
Fit the upper and lower retaining screws (1) and tighten them to the torque of 25 Nm ±5%.
Fit the glove compartment (5) and side body panels to rear subframe parts (2) and (3), and tighten screws (4) to 18 Nm ±10%.
Make sure adhesive pads (A) are in place at the indicated points of glove compartment (5) and ensure they are positioned so that their lower profile is tangent to the upper edge of poppets (B) and centred with respect to each poppet vertical axis, as shown.
Fit the electrical components compartment (V) together with the relevant components.
Route wiring properly, making reference to the wiring tables (Routing of wiring on frame) for the base version.
Route wiring properly, making reference to the wiring tables (Routing of wiring on frame) for the Pikes Peak, Granturismo and Touring versions.
