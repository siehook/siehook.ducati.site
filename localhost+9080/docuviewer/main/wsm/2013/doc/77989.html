Refitting the tank fairings
Make sure that clips (8) are in place on central tank fairing (7).
Fit the safety tab of hands free cover (9) in the relevant hole on central tank fairing (7)
Install the hands free cover (9) to the central tank fairing (7) by pressing in the direction indicated by the arrows.
Refit central tank fairing (7), start and tighten the front screws (3), with nylon washers, to a torque of 4 Nm ±10%.
Warning
The rear screw must be started in its thread, fully home but not tightened.
Make sure that clips (8) are in place on tank fairings (5) and (6).
Refit the LH side (5) and RH side (6) tank fairing as follows: start screws (2) after fitting the corresponding washers.
Start the screws (4).
Start and tighten the screws (10) to a torque of 4 Nm ± 10%.
Tighten the screws (4) to a torque of 4 Nm ± 10% and the screws (2) to a torque of 2 Nm ± 10%.
Start the tank fairing to headlight fairing retaining screws (1) and tighten them to a torque of 2 Nm ±10%.
Refit the front half-fairing (Refitting the front half-fairing) and the left and right-hand side panel covers (Refitting the panel).
