Removing the hand guards
Remove the panel (Removing the panel).
Remove the seat (Removing the seat).
Remove the front half-fairings (Removing the front half-fairing).
Remove the tank fairings (Removing the tank fairings).
Hand guard removal is the same for both.
Following is the procedure for the RH hand guard (1).
Remove the rear-view mirrors as explained under Section "Removing the rear-view mirrors".
Undo and remove the screws (2) securing the hand guard to the clamp.
Loosen and remove the securing screw (3) recovering the vibration damper (4) and nut (5).
Undo the screw (6) and collect the spacer (7).
Disconnect front turn indicator connector (D) as described under Section "Routing of wiring on frame" for the base version or under Section "Routing of wiring on frame" for the Pikes Peak, Granturismo or Touring versions and remove the complete RH hand guard.
If necessary, disassemble hand guard by removing screws (8).
